# Privacy Policy for ExactlyApps

Effective Date: December 2025

ExactlyApps respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile applications handle user information.

## Information Collection and Use

ExactlyApps applications do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

We do not require users to create accounts, sign in, or provide personal information to use our apps.

## Data Storage

All data entered into ExactlyApps applications is stored locally on the user’s device only. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

## Analytics and Advertising

ExactlyApps applications do not use analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## Permissions

Some apps may request system permissions (such as notifications or storage access) strictly to provide core app functionality. These permissions are not used to collect personal data.

## Third-Party Services

ExactlyApps applications do not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

## Children’s Privacy

ExactlyApps applications do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if app functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
